Brand Story

Two colors. One promise.

Our brand identity reflects who we are — a bridge between the warmth of travel and the precision of technology.

Yeyak is the Korean word for "reservation." We chose it because it captures exactly what we do — and because our roots run deep in a culture that values precision and hospitality in equal measure.

The Yeyak symbol is built from the letter "Y" — a checkmark-like form that represents confirmation, completion, and the satisfying moment when a complex booking clicks into place.

From day one, we knew that orange was our color. It carries the warmth, energy, and excitement that define great travel experiences. As our platform evolved into a SaaS product serving enterprise clients, we introduced blue — a signal of the trust, accuracy, and seamless reliability that B2B partners demand.

Together, these colors embody our dual promise: the joy of travel, delivered with enterprise-grade precision.

Yeyak Orange

The warmth and energy of travel. Excitement, discovery, human connection — everything that makes this industry worth transforming.

Yeyak Blue

Trust, precision, and technology. The reliable backbone of seamless B2B operations — accuracy that travel partners can count on.
